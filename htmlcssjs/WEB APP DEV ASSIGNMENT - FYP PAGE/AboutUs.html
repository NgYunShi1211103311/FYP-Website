About Us

The FYP System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the final year project (FYP) experience for students. It connects students with experienced supervisors who provide guidance and support throughout the project lifecycle.

By leveraging the FYP System, students can enhance their project management skills, improve their research capabilities, and ultimately deliver high-quality final year projects.
